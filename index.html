FAQs
How is the Quality Bonus calculated?
Quality Bonus is based on your average client rating (out of 5.0) and technique score (out of 100). Both scores are averaged and multiplied by your tier multiplier.
When does Social Virality Bonus unlock?
The Social Virality Bonus unlocks 7 days after a reel is posted, once view and save thresholds are confirmed by the platform analytics team.
How do I redeem my bonus?
Tap "Redeem" on the Portfolio page. The amount will be transferred to your linked bank account within 2–3 business days.
What happens if I change tiers?
Tier changes apply from the next calendar month. Your current month's bonuses will be calculated at your existing tier rate.
